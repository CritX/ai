AI Alpha 1.0.3
----------
Normal commands:
/help: opens the help prompt
/debug [value]: starts debug mode
/exit: exits out of the program, updating the sentence bank
----------
Debug commands:
/phrases: shows the list of sentences in the bank
/len: shows the number of values in the bank
/clear: clears the log and bank, be careful
/log: shows the AI log
/len log: shows the number of values in the log
